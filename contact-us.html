Pet Friendly Policy

We understand that pets are an important part of the family. We are pet friendly by arrangement
- so please talk to us about your needs. We offer a fully enclosed backyard, shady trees, water bowls, food bowl
and a lead if you forget one. Only 3 minutes from a leash free beach, an oval close by and if you want to sleep in
we are happy to take your dog (depending on its size) for a walk in the morning.
